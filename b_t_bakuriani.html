Kazbegi

3 Day Tour in kazbegi, Stefanwminda

DAY 1

 At the morning after the  breakfast we will pack all our bicycle and bags in the car, our firs destination is Bazaleti lake.  after the one and half hour transfer we will set on our bicycle saddle and there starts our ride on old army road.
after the 15 kilometres and two hour we will be at the top point of our ride and have some break on the Shadow of the biggest tree on the mountain,  which is standing there several hundred years like a king of the forest. After the 10 kilometres and one hour downhill we will be at the finish line of ride day 1 in Ananuri  Castle arounding also Jinvali lake.  then we will Pack bikes in the car and after one hour transfer we will enjoy amazing Gudauri mountains  wievs. then we don't have any break until Stepantsminda  where we can see the misty Mountains Cold. Mount Yazbegi 5036 meters height.  in the evening of two beautiful day we will have dinner in stepantsminda wiev off mountain kazbegi.

DAY 2

 At the morning you think that misty Mountains cold it was just dream, but when you go to the balcony over Friendly guest house you understand that this is real, he's standing still there and it's never getting to be boring to watch it. after the tasty breakfast we will say thanks to housekeepers and then starts our 1:30min riding to Gergeti Sameba church where you will enjoying the wiev of stepantsminda and the high Rocks, then we will go back to the fast Downhill and there starts some short transfer to our second ride of the day. maybe you think that you have seen all the misty Mountains but at the single trek of Chaukhebi Lake there you'll see another full-time snow white mountain, there we will have some break on the lake which is the freezing cold even during the summer time, and there are some brave guys who sweem there,and have some lunch then we will go all Downhill or the singletrack, where we will finish our ride. At the end of one of the beautiful day of your life we will have dinner in the restaurant with the misty mountains cold Kazbegi Glacier.

DAY 3

 When you wake up you will feel glacier is calling you and you will go to the balcony,  but when you see it you are sad because it is the last day of our trip.  after the breakfast we will say goodbye to housekeeper who is too familiar to us because for her hospitality.  we will have short transfer to starting point of our treking we will see amazing walleys on our single track way of waterfalls. after going back to the car, we will have short transfer, and then ride with bike into the Walley Truso, there we will see and drink mineral Waters which is coming from the ground, and also we can see how is coming the whole river from land Under the Mountain there we will have some break and have a lunch in the nature.
then we will go to the transfer car, into the Tbilisi, then I will let you to rest in the hotel but it is not the end then we will use  cable car and we will have dinner on  very popular place Mtatsminda mountain where is the amazing wiev of Tbilisi especially at night.
