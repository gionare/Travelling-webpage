About Us

❮
❯

If you love to ride, you’ve come to the right place. Nowhere else in Georgia you can ride with Georgian MTB, Road race and Track race National Champions. And we offer so much more than just the bike ride.
Along with expert trail and trip advice, our professional guides can lead you or your group through stunning forests or terrain to satisfy any level. Bikes, gear, workshop, bike-wash and more – we’ve got everything to make sure your ride is a smooth and pleasurable one.
So if you are looking for traveling and want to experience our incredible countryside or regions from two wheels – you’ve come to the right place.
