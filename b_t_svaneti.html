Svaneti

5 Day Tour in Svaneti, Mestia, Ushguli

DAY 1

We need about 7-8 hours from tbilisi to Svaneti, so it's better to start our ride as early as possible after bike and bags pack in the car we will have breackfast and start our road, where we will stop for several times, for coffe of course, for dinner and for sataplia cave wich is 900 m long, 10 m high and 12 m wide and contains dramatically lit stalactites and stalagmites, olso some preserved Dinosaur Footprints, then we will walk on A glass-bottomed viewing platform, after that we have one stop for Enguri Dam, It is the world's second highest concrete arch dam with a height of 271.5 metres. This dam is feeding both Georgia and Abkhasia. then our destination is Mestia.

DAY 2

Wake up in the morning, now we have to go up in Hatsvali mountain, by car untill the cable car it's 9 kilometers so who is brave enough like me, we can also start riding from bottom, and fight to the steep mountains.
After cable car, on Mount Hatsvali there is very nice view, you can see Ushba mountain wich is more than 4900 meters from sea level, we will stop there for a while take some coffe ans snacks and From there we will start full time Downhill on the single tracks and doubletracks really enjoy with it. than we will go bach to our guest house. After that we will go in to the Koruldi lake by car, wich is very beautifull.

DAY 3

At the morning we will have breackfast and start our from Mestia to Chalaadi glacer at first we have to go 20 kilometers and then keep going by foot, the road to the glacer will be, very beautifull at first we have to walk through the forest and than on rochy uphils following the little path, the top point is glacer wich is full time ice many many centuries, and water is comming from it, there we will have a snacks, after that we will come back into the Mestia where we will have a dinner, after dinner we will start riding with our bikes wich are clean, our Guides always likde clean bikes, We have to ride onto the other past of Svaneti, Ushguli.

DAY 4

Wake up in the morning. starting our ride with bicycles. Our way is going near to themount Shkhara wich is 5208 meters hight, highest in Georgia and second in Caucasus Region. But as you guess we aren't going up to there, our road is mostly flat, following the glacier river wich is getting smaller and smaller on our way. after 15 kilometers we have to stop, leave there bicycles and start hiking about 5-7 kilometers. Coming back to Ushguli we have enouf time to take enother hiking hours up to Q.Tamars old castle, were she was living and hiding from enemy.

DAY 5

Ushguli - Lentekhi - Tbilisi ..... 15k uphill, 3 times more Downhill
We will have breackfast and start to fill our cars with our bags, and start riding into the 13.5kilometers uphill in to the Zagaro pass We are discending there Where only jeep's can ride no road for normal sedans. You are riding your bike going down and in front of you, there is a huge mountain, all from 4 directions huge mountains are looking at you, Sometimes we can see there deers, running on the green grass, so this way will be unforgottable for you, the glacier water wich we crossed easily on the top, now it is huge nonstoppable river going down with his sounds, after the ride we can sweem in the another small river, clean our bikes, relax, have a dinner and go to Tbilisi again.
