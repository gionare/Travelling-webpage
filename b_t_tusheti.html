5 Day Tour in Tusheti

DAY 1

 At the morning, in Tbilisi, after the breakfast we will pack all our bikes and bags in our car and our Trip starts. After 2 hours transfer we will set on our bicycles and our first ride starts there to the top of abano path40km 3000 meters elevation. at the top we can see from up side how eagles fly. then we have 15 km downhill and 15 km flats to the Omalo, there is waiting us verry friendly guesthouse.and first day ride is done. at the evening we will have traditional dinner in the guesthouse. after dinner we can enjoy beautiful wiew of tusheti mountains and the sky full of stars.

DAY 2

 After the tasty breakfast we will start our day 2 ride. the first destination is kesero towers at the same time we will see the beautiful wiew from the mountain and the towers which was protecting the womans and children during the rival attacks then we will go to village diklo and there starts very nice single track to the border of Dagestani (russian federation). we will have som picnic there on the fields, then we will come back to our guesthouse in Omalo. After the finish of ride day 2 we will wash our bicycles, ourselves and have some rest untill draditional dinner, After the Dinner we will join the wiew of nature.

DAY 3

 Atthe mornig in Omalo after the breakfast we will pack all our bags in the car and we will start our day 3 ride, After 10 km ride on the path we have some break on the balkony of Tusheti wiew. Then we have double track uphill to the Bochorna village where starts 15 Km single track with little technical segments and amazing wiews of Caucasus mountains then we have 7 km doubletrack downhill undil village Dartlo which is our final dastination of day 3 ride. And there is waiting us very friendly guesthouse. After the shower and washing bikes we will have tasty dinner . After the dinner we will see beautiful sunset betwen the mountains.


DAY 4

 At the morning of day 4 in Tusheti we will have sime breakfast and we can enjoy the morning. After that we set on our bikes and we have some easy ride to Girevi, mostly flats.on the road we can see river tunnels throught the snow even in the summer time. Untill the girevi we have single track along the river. There in Girevi we will have some picnic in nature, And then we wil come back to Dartlo. Where we finish ou day 4 rideand we can enjoy the wiew of night mountains. at the evening we will have traditional dinner.

DAY 5

 At the early morning we have breakfast, then we pack all our bags in the car and we set in the bike saddles and we start our ride to the abano path where we can see again from upside how eagles fly. then we have 40 km downhill untill the finish line of day 5 ride. the we will wash our bikes and have some lunch. then we will sit in the car and have transfer to the Tbilisi hotel. there we will rest some time and at the evening we will go by cableway to the mtatsminda mountain and enjoy the wiew of night city during the dinner.
